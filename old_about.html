THE STUDIO

Teagher Studio

During our studies, we came together to the conclusion that we wanted to make our own games. We were always dreaming about it, and were amazed by all the other indie game studios. And we asked us one question: "Why not giving it a try?" And Teagher Studio was born.


The first steps weren't the easiest, but anyway, we made it there... At first it was only Lea and Thomas working on small and weird games, we were beginners but it was still cool. A few years later, Anatole joined us as we were working on increasingly bigger games. After a little while, we needed a few more people, that's when Jean-Christophe joined, promptly followed by Paul, eager to discover the world of video game creation with us.


We're quite happy to be able to work for what we dream about, and thus we try to deliver the best games we can. We focus on really small games on which we can spend a lot of time on polishing everything. We'd rather have a more polished and smaller game than a big clunky one. We experiment new things, make weird games, but we're incredibly proud of it.
