Pitch

Run, Climb, Fall and Retry - A fair platformer about running and jumping through a huge minimalistic tower made from the tears of our playtesters. Nothing is unfair, keep trying and you will succeed.

Description

ULTRA VERTIGO is all about running and jumping, as you try to get to the finish line as fast as possible. Based on fair platforming and precise jumping, it is all about your skills.

Features

  • RUN RUN RUN RUN

    ULTRA VERTIGO is a game about well... running. Be fast, be nimble, stay focused and get to the end as fast as you can. Modifiers and secrets will keep things fresh, go out there and explore!

  • CLIMB CLIMB CLIMB CLIMB

    Jump and climb as high as possible. The finish line lies at the very top of this huge vertical level. We hope you are not afraid of heights!

  • FALL FALL FALL FALL

    Yes, indeed, you will fall. Quite a lot. Of course, your legs are pretty strong, no fall damage or anything like that. The only damage you get from falling is emotional, as the level scrolls in front of you, showing all the progress you are losing while falling.

  • RETRY RETRY RETRY RETRY

    Keep running, keep climbing, keep falling, keep... retrying. Somewhere down the line you will succeed. It is only a matter of time, sweat and tears. Though, getting to the end of the level is only the beginning, as you can improve your time, discover new routes and find shortcuts!
