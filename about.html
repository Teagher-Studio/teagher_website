Co-Founder / Artistic Director

Teagher Studio

During our studies, we came together to the conclusion that we wanted to make our own games. We were always dreaming about it, and were amazed by all the other indie game studios. And we asked us one question: "Why not giving it a try?" And Teagher Studio was born.


The first steps weren't the easiest, but anyway, we made it there... And you're reading this! We love games that have an amazing, creative action gameplay (well.. you'll probably never see a strategy game from us.. but anyway), and beautiful universes, with a nice and original homemade art-style.


We're quite happy to be able to work for what we dream about, and thus we try to deliver the best games we can. We take our time to ensure the maximum quality. We're not releasing a lot of games, as we prefer to keep polishing our games until we're satisfied, choosing quality over quantity. We're here on an incredible journey, and we're stoked to show our creations to the world!
